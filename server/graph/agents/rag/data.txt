--- APPOINTMENT POLICIES ---

- Cardiology appointments require a referral from a general practitioner.
- Dermatology appointments can be booked directly.
- All appointments require patient full name, phone number, and email.
- Maximum of one appointment per patient per day per specialty.
- Emergency cases override normal scheduling rules.

--- EMERGENCY INSTRUCTIONS ---

- Chest pain, unconsciousness, severe bleeding: Call emergency services immediately.
- Minor skin irritation: Schedule a dermatology appointment.
- High fever (>39°C): Schedule a general medicine appointment.
- Severe allergic reaction: Call emergency services immediately.

--- PATIENT PREPARATION GUIDES ---

Blood Tests:
- Fasting required for 8-12 hours before blood tests.
- Drink water only, avoid coffee or tea.
- Bring ID and insurance card.

Dermatology Consultation:
- Note any current medications or creams used.
- Take photos of skin issues if possible.

Cardiology Consultation:
- Bring prior test results (ECG, echocardiogram).
- List current medications.
- Avoid caffeine 2 hours before the appointment.

General Checkup:
- Bring vaccination history.
- List any chronic conditions or medications.
